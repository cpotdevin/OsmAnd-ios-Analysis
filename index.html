OsmAnd iOS Analysis

Andres David Laiton, Christian Potdevin

Description:

OsmAnd Maps is a map application with access to the free, worldwide, and high-quality OpenStreetMap (OSM) data. All map data can be stored on the device's memory for offline use. All the main functionalities work both online and offline (no internet needed). The user can decide which maps want to download by country, besides United States that are per state. In the free version up to 3 maps can be downloaded and have in app purchases for premium services such as more maps downloaded. It uses the OpenStreetMap (OSM) map database for its primary displays, but is an independent app not endorsed by the OpenStreetMap Foundation. This is the official website. This app is available for Android and iOS devices.


This review is about the free iOS app made in Swift. Its the version 3.00 of the app that has a 3.5 rating over 5 based on 58 ratings. According to the information available in the App Store, the app has a size of 255.5 MB, requires iOS 9.3 or later and it's compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch. It is important to clarify that the app has some libraries made in Objective-C, Objective-C++, C, C++ and Python. Some of these libraries are from Cocoa Pods.
